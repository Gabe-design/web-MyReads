Author: Temple Grandin

Twenty-five years ago, author and educator Temple Grandin changed mainstream understanding of autism with her memoir, Thinking in Pictures. Her new book expands on the theme, celebrating visual/spatial cognition, intuitive design, and the critical importance of those who think differently.
